HOW WE WORK

From brief to handover, in four stages.

The order matters — each stage is signed off before the next begins, so there are no surprises mid-build.

01

Consult

We walk the space, talk through how you'll use it, and scope budget and timeline before anything is drawn.

02

Design

Layouts, materials and 3D visuals are refined with you until the plan is one you're confident signing off.

03

Build

Our own site teams execute the fit-out to a fixed schedule, with weekly progress updates — no chasing required.

04

Handover

A final walkthrough, snagging resolved on the spot where possible, and documentation for everything installed.
