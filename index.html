HOW WE WORK

From brief to handover, in four stages.

The order matters — each stage is signed off before the next begins, so there are no surprises mid-build.

01

Consult

We walk the space, understand how it will be used, and scope the work against budget and timeline.

02

Design

Ideas are converted into construction-ready designs — materials, layouts and MEP planned before anything is built.

03

Build

Our own teams handle civil works, MEP, joinery, metalwork, paint and flooring — one crew, no subcontractor handoffs.

04

Handover

A final walkthrough, snagging resolved on the spot where possible, and documentation for everything installed.
